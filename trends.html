What trends in web design are going to hit this next year? We do a quick review of the most important ones to keep up to date.

We bring you a compilation of the main web design trends that we will see in 2020.

  • Dark Mode

    Dark mode is so hot right now.

    Nothing super innovative but we are seeing it everywhere and it is one of the clearest trends if not the most important.

  • 3D everywhere

    We already started to see it last year and it is getting bigger and bigger. The design gains perspective and is translated into new representations.

  • Fonts without rules

    We are in the era where designers break the traditional rules of typography. Time to play.

    Examples:

    • Elim Chan
    • Huffington Post

  • Your own personality

    Everyone has access to stock images, to unsplash or whatever. And for this reason, it is increasingly necessary to have your own visual personality. Illustrations that convey your brand in a unique way.

    Examples:

    • Intercom
    • Mailchimp

  • Animation

    It is clear that today, more than ever, we need to capture the attention of our audience. Therefore, animate backgrounds, shapes, images, etc. It is almost a must if we want to live up to expectations.

    Examples:

    • 5rue
    • Achos
    • Festival Francia Corta
    • Google Cloud

  • Anti-standard. The search for the eclectic

    Having a "pretty" design today is very easy. You just have to download a template from one of the hundreds of template websites on the market.

    However, differentiation is vital, more and more, and that is why we must avoid, as much as possible, standards and repeat formulas. We have to surprise our audience.

    Examples:

    • Arche68
    • Achos
